💡 Recommended Pricing Ranges

🟢 Competitive (1 - 10,000 uarkeo/req)

Most providers in this range attract high volume. Good for providers with spare capacity who want consistent subscribers.

  • 1K requests: 0.01 - 0.1 ARKEO
  • 1M requests: 10 - 100 ARKEO
  • Best for: New providers, high-volume chains

🟡 Standard (10,000 - 100,000 uarkeo/req)

Reasonable for established providers with good uptime and specialized chains. Comparable to centralized alternatives.

  • 1K requests: 0.1 - 1 ARKEO
  • 1M requests: 100 - 1,000 ARKEO
  • Best for: Specialized services, archive nodes

🔴 Premium (100,000+ uarkeo/req)

High rates only viable for unique or hard-to-access chains where no alternatives exist. Most subscribers will look elsewhere.

  • 1K requests: 1+ ARKEO
  • 1M requests: 1,000+ ARKEO
  • Best for: Rare chains, exclusive data

⚙️ How Billing Works

1

Open a Contract

You deposit ARKEO tokens into a smart contract with a specific provider. The deposit is your spending budget. No subscription, no recurring charge.

2

Sign Each Request

Every RPC call you make is signed with your wallet key and includes an incrementing nonce. The provider verifies your signature before serving data.

3

Pay Per Request

Each request deducts the provider's rate from your deposit. The provider claims payment on-chain periodically. You see exactly how much you've spent.

4

Close Anytime

Close your contract at any time to reclaim unspent funds. No lock-in period. Your remaining deposit is returned to your wallet.

Subscription vs Pay-As-You-Go

Arkeo supports both models. PAYG contracts let you pay per request with no minimum commitment. Subscription contracts give you a fixed rate for a set duration. Most providers offer PAYG. Check the provider detail page for subscription options.
